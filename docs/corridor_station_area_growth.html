Trend Scenario

The following series of charts display anticipated employment and dwelling unit densities by year in Polk County in both the “trend” and “TOD” land use scenarios.

The “trend” scenario is comprised of the Polk TPO 2045 LRTP Land Use Forecast.

The “TOD” scenario is based on the 2045 LRTP land use, but assumes an intensified amount of dwelling unit and job growth occurs within proposed station areas.

These plots reveal how the exiting and future year densities compare to minimum BRT thresholds in “A Framework for Transit Oriented Development in Florida.” In addition, mean corridor typology values from “TCRP 187: Livable Transit Corridors: Methods, Metrics, Strategies”, and “DRPT Multimodal Design Guidelines” are included for the employment charts. Similar values are not available for dwelling unit figures, as TCRP 187 uses population as a metric.
